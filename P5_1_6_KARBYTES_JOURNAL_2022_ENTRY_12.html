15_AUGUST_2022: Earlier this month I found a video on Twitter which depicted an animated duck which was a busy software developer who appeared to be taking a break at a photorealistic rendition of Lake Merrit in Oakland, California. I like how that duck is always alone and can hardly stand to take a break from working on code. I downloaded that video and then uploaded it to my media library on the Internet Archive (i.e. Archive dot Org).
